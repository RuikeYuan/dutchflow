Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Dutch Frequency Trainer is a vocabulary study app for learning Dutch. The app is designed to keep study data on your device whenever possible.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app may store your notebook words, study progress, generated examples, saved translations, and interface preferences locally on your device. This data is used only to provide the learning experience.

Microphone And Speech Recognition

If you use speaking practice, the app may request microphone and speech recognition access. This is used only when you start speaking practice so the app can compare your spoken Dutch with practice text.

AI Features

Optional AI features, such as generating example sentences or translating examples, require an online service. When enabled, the sentence or prompt needed for that feature may be sent to the configured AI service. The app does not sell personal information.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app developer through the support channel listed on the support page.
